AI Meeting Optimizer In development

AI-powered meeting optimization tool that automatically transcribes audio recordings, analyzes content, and generates intelligent summaries and action items.

Leverages advanced AI models for natural language processing and audio transcription. The system will be capable of understanding context, identifying important decisions, and automatically generating actionable insights from meeting recordings.

Designed to significantly reduce the time spent on meeting follow-ups and ensure that important decisions and action items are never missed or forgotten. The platform incorporates cutting-edge technology to streamline business communication processes.

Technologies Used
Java Spring Boot AI/ML APIs HTML5/CSS3 JavaScript PostgreSQL
Key Features
  • Audio-to-text transcription using AI
  • Automatic meeting summary generation
  • Action item extraction and assignment
  • Speaker identification and time tracking
  • Key topic and decision point highlighting
  • Integration with calendar and task management
Business Impact

Designed to significantly reduce the time spent on meeting follow-ups and ensure that important decisions and action items are never missed or forgotten.
